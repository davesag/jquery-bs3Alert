A Very simple test of the bs3Alert jQuery Plugin

Click on the button below and be amazed as the alert appears. Then click on the alert's close box and tremble as it vanishes. Then click on the button again and gaze on in wonder as the alear reappears.

It's a standard Bootstrap 3 Alert

Which means you can bind alert close events just like normal, but with the advantage that the alert can be easily re-opened. This makes it excellent for displaying errors on web pages that are the result of ajax calls.
